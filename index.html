Located in the heart of Maryland's horse country, Manor Tavern, also known as The Tavern, offers a relaxed atmosphere, superior service, and flavorful meals that feature locally grown and sourced ingredients. The Tavern also offers a tremendous craft cocktail menu, entertainment space for various gatherings, and catering services.
